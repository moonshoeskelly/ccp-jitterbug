Our Story

Country Chic Paint was developed, and launched, in 2014 by husband-and-wife team Jan and Roseanne Korteland. Even as their team has grown they continue to have a friendly, family-business feel and company culture and retailers are seen as true partners.

All Country Chic Paint products are thoughtfully designed and environmentally conscious. With a devotion to sustainability, environmental friendliness, and a positive work culture Country Chic Paint strives to improve not just the spaces you live in but the communities we are part of as well.
